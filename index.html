Why This Directory Exists

Safe Water Southwest exists because the Arizona water treatment market is full of noise. Franchise systems, national call centers, and unlicensed contractors make it hard to find someone who actually knows your local water.

This directory curates specialists with verified Arizona licenses, documented service areas, and a track record in your community. Every listing is reviewed before inclusion. Every listing meets the vetting criteria above. Vendors cannot pay to bypass these requirements, though paid placements above the directory floor will be clearly disclosed.

What "Vetted" Means Here

  • 📋Active Arizona ROC License — Arizona Registrar of Contractors license confirmed and in good standing. Publicly searchable at roc.az.gov.
  • 📍Documented Arizona Service Area — Vendor has confirmed specific Arizona cities or regions they serve. National call centers without local presence are excluded.
  • 🏢Verifiable Business Address — Physical Arizona business location confirmed. P.O. boxes and virtual offices do not qualify.
  • ⭐Independent Review Presence — Vendor has an established presence on at least one independent review platform (Google, Yelp, or BBB) with a documented service history.
